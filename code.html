About Us

Welcome to Five Star Fast Food! We have a total of three branches in our Little Red Dot. We started our business in Taman Jurong in the 1980s, expanded north to open our second branch in Marsiling in the late 2000s, and recently brought our specialties to Kampong Glam at the end of 2023.

Our team is passionate about providing delicious Indian 'Mamak' cuisines to our valued customers. Located at Golden Mile Hawker Centre, Beach Road, our menu is carefully crafted to include a variety of mouth-watering dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings.
